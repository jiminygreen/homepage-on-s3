Health insurance

  • Confused by health insurance? Call 13 29 39 for a Health Cover Check to help you find cover that meets your individual needs.
  • Switching from another fund? Join within 30 days of leaving your old fund and we’ll recognise waiting periods you’ve already completed so you can claim sooner.
  • Want to keep seeing your favourite physio or chiro? You can get treated by any health provider who is in private practice and recognised by Australian Unity.
